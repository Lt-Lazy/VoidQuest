What is VoidQuest?

VoidQuest is a retro-style RPG filled with unique creatures, mysterious NPCs, and a world full of secrets.

This wiki provides an overview of everything from fish and monsters to locations, characters, tips, information and the story behind the game.

Tips!

Movement and interaction:

The game is played by moving around with "w", "s", "a" and "d".

You can use your mouse to click on buttons and "e" to interact.

"j" opens journal, "m" opens map, "i", opens inventory, "c" opens character interface and "TAB" opens menu.
